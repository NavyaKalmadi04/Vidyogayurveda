About VidYoga

Where Ancient Wisdom Meets Modern Healing

VidYoga Ayurveda was founded on the principle that true health is a balance of body, mind, and spirit. Rooted in the 5,000-year-old science of Ayurveda and the discipline of classical Yoga, we bring personalised wellness to every individual.

Our motto — विद्या ददाति विनयम् (Knowledge gives humility) — guides everything we do. We believe in educating our students deeply so they can carry the wisdom of these ancient traditions into their daily lives.
